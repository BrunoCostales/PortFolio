About Me

I’m a web developer trained at a private university in Argentina. During my studies, I earned a scholarship at a well-known company in my country, where I helped develop a website as part of a team. The project was carried out synchronously, following agile methodologies such as Scrum, with sprints, deadlines, and regular meetings that allowed us to organize and deliver efficiently.

I worked as a Technical Analyst for an official courier company in Argentina, where I supported over 4,000 users, managed Unix/Linux environments, and developed scripts using PowerShell and CMD. This role also involved regular interaction with people, which strengthened my communication and customer support skills.

I lived in Aspen, Colorado, where I developed custom digital solutions for local businesses. I am currently based in Argentina, continuing to work on personal projects and expanding my knowledge. I work with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, MySQL, PHP, WordPress, Bootstrap, Git, GitHub, and scripting tools. I’m committed to continuous learning to deliver high-quality, valuable solutions.
